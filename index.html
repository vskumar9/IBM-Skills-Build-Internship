About Pixel Paradise

Pixel Paradise is more than a center for gamers of all ages and interests. We have top of the line PCs, the latest next-generation consoles, including PlayStation 5's, Xbox series X and S's, and Nintendo Switches, HTC Vive and Oculus Quest 2 Virtual Reality (VR) headsets, and immersive racing and flight simulators, all with a large library of games included to play, at a very affordable price. 


We share a space with Omar's World of Comics and Omar's Bistro, which is owned by a more than exceptional young business owner with Down Syndrome. With him, we share the same vision of outreach and inclusivity of family, community, and people with disabilities. 


To read more about Omar's World of Comics products and events, please click here.

To read more about Omar's Bistro, please click here.


We have the best staff who love gaming themselves and at our core, we are here to provide a safe space to build and grow our gaming community. Video games can cause people to become increasingly detached from each other in the physical sense, and playing alone at home does not compare to the excitement that comes from playing games with people who share the same interests as you, in the same physical space.
